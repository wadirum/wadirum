Wadi Rum Tours

Explore the desert and the Bedouin lifestyle by jeep, camel or on foot. Enjoy a tea, eat local food and sleep under the stars!

Epic Experience

Wadi Rum (Arabic: وادي رم‎‎) is a valley cut into the sandstone and granite rock in southern Jordan 60 km (37 mi) to the east of Aqaba in Jordan

Wadi Rum is the highlight of most tourists and backpackers in Jordan.

It's breathtaking scenery was used as film locations for the classic Lawrence of Arabia and more recently on Transformers: Revenge of the Fallen, Rogue One: A Star Wars Story and many others.
